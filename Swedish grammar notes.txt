Fundament: represents start of a sentence, it can be a subject or a adverb representing time and place

Verb1 : In Swedish there is a rule V2 which means the first verb in a sentence will always come in second place. Verb1 can be a normal verb or a helper verb
Subjekt : This is optional and subject will be included in this position only if Fundament  is not a subject

Sats Adverbial : a special adverb(sentence adverb) which modifies or changes the meaning of an entire sentence
e.g 
inte (not)
kanske (maybe)
ofta (often)
alltid (always)
tyvärr (unfortunately)
aldrig (never)
